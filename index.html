I am a PhD Scholar in Economics. My research focuses on International Finance, Capital Controls, Financial Crises, Markov Switching Models, and Early Warning Systems.

My current research examines the relationship between capital controls and macroeconomic variables using cross-spectral analysis and investigates extreme capital flow regimes in India using a Markov Switching Model with Time-Varying Transition Probabilities.
